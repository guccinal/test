How to create Safe Transaction?

To initiate a Safe Transaction you must connect your wallet with NFT, or the
amount you are willing to trade for. Do this by clicking Connect Wallet button in
top right. You have successfully logged into your wallet, choose the NFT and
amount you are trading for.
